Pitch and Rhythm in Speech and Music (PRiSM) Project

We will soon be seeking adolescent participants (aged 11-15) for a project exploring music and language development. This is a behavioral study in which adolescents will hear a series of musical excerpts and spoken phrases and be asked to place them into categories.
